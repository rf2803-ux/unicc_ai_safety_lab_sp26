# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, inclusive, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and respectful language.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Provide actionable feedback.
- Respect privacy, security boundaries, and responsible disclosure.
- Be careful when discussing AI safety risks or potentially sensitive examples.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private credentials, secrets, or personal data.
- Encouraging unsafe, destructive, or unauthorized testing.
- Publicly disclosing security issues before maintainers have a chance to respond.

## Enforcement

Before public release, this section should be updated with the official maintainer contact and escalation process.
